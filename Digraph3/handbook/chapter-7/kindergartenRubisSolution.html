Rubis family of criteria.

# Identifyer Name Comment Weight Scale Thresholds
direction min max indifference weak preference preference weak veto veto
1 ACC Accessibility to Madrid city centre In minutes. The metropolitan area of Madrid covers a wide area, causing transportation accessibility to be of importantance when choosing a site for a new facility. In a city with heavy traffic congestion, the actual distance might not correlate with accessibility, and therefore we have chosen to measure it in minutes by public transportation from a central transport hub. 0.30 min 30.00 60.00 6.50 12.50
2 COP land and construction prize The sites have differing costs of location and construction depending on the building location (e.g. residential zone, city center or outskirts). 0.30 min 3000000.00 5500000.00 10000.00 100000.00
3 EFF Effects to the city landscape Government regulations define that effects to urban landscape, green spaces, and cultural heritage must be estimated. We measure them with odinal ranking number from best (1rst) to worst (7th). 0.10 min 1.00 7.00
4 MAC maintenance costs of the facility In euros / month, measured by estimating total fixed and variable costs (supplies, personnel, taxes, etc). 0.10 min 20000.00 50000.00 0.00 + -0.03x 0.00 + -0.08x
5 SIZ Size (in children) of the kindergarten size (in children) of the kindergarten to be built as measured with a number of day-care places. The Spanish government regulates \citep{BOE:2005,BOE:2007} the maximum number of children for $m^2$ and the required common services. The number of day-care places can be derived from the estimated building specifications. 0.20 max 100.00 300.00 1.50 3.00
